impactful projects

Today we are working on 16 different environment related projects around the state of New York and are actively looking for more people to join our team.

passionate team

We're a lot more than a group of environmentalist. We're a team that is commited to helping the environment and the communities around us.

With our efforts and with the help of other organisations and communities around the state, we've seen a significant improvement when it comes to pollution and laws to protect the only planet that we have.

We couldn't be happier after all the positive work that has been conducted by Wicker Park over the past 2 decades.

our projects

Our main focus has always been the Wicker Park, this park is not only located in Buffalo, but most of our team members has a strong connection with this park. Most of us have lived in Buffalo all our lives, and we feel very strongly about protecting our city's environment.

Wicker Park - Protecting the Wild Life

Because wicker Park is located in the middle of the city, there aren't that many species. However, it's very important that we protect the few that still inhabits among us. We work very hard on feeding and providing shelter to different bird and duck species.
